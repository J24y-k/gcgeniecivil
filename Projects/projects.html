Kolwezi Road Upgrade

Location: Kolwezi, DRC • Duration: 6 months • Services: Roadworks, Drainage, Reticulation

Scope: Full reconstruction and rehabilitation of 4.2 km of municipal road, including subgrade rework, new layerworks, kerbing and stormwater management with attenuation works and gabion protection in critical sections.

Outcome: Improved flood resilience, reduced travel times for local traffic and measurable reduction in maintenance calls in the first 12 months. Delivered within budget and with coordinated stakeholder engagement.

Sandton Stormwater Works

Location: Sandton, Gauteng • Duration: 4 months • Services: Attenuation Ponds, Reticulation

Scope: Constructed two attenuation ponds, full reticulation and final surfacing for a commercial precinct. Included erosion control and sediment management during construction.

Outcome: Controlled runoff, reduced local street flooding and improved stormwater conveyance for adjacent properties.

Kinshasa Bulk Earthworks

Location: Kinshasa, DRC • Duration: 5 months • Services: Bulk Earthworks, Platform Formation

Scope: Large-scale platform formation for a commercial development: cut and fill operations, compaction control and haul optimisation, with environmental management during works.

Outcome: Material management plan saved client 18% on haul costs; platform completed to tolerance and ready for immediate substructure works.

Randburg Housing Platform

Location: Randburg, Johannesburg • Duration: 3 months • Services: Bulk Earthworks, Reticulation

Scope: Cut-to-fill platform formation, service trenches for water and sewer, and road tie-ins for a high-density housing project.

Outcome: Fast mobilisation and delivery allowed follow-on trades to begin early, reducing overall programme by three weeks.

Battersea Utilities Upgrade

Location: Battersea, London • Duration: 5 months • Services: Utilities, Roadworks
